Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Call us directly — we're happy to chat.

Does Drain Pro Plus offer free estimates? +
Yes — Drain Pro Plus always provides free estimates before any work begins. We'll assess your plumbing issue thoroughly and give you a clear, upfront price with no hidden fees or surprises. You only pay when you're comfortable with the quote.
Do you offer same-day service? +
Absolutely. We know that plumbing problems can't always wait. In most cases we can schedule same-day or next-day service. For urgent situations like backed-up sewer lines or active leaks, call us directly at (410) 555-0123 and we'll prioritize your appointment.
What areas does Drain Pro Plus serve? +
We're based in Essex, MD and serve all of Baltimore County including Baltimore City, Middle River, White Marsh, Dundalk, Towson, Rosedale, Parkville, Perry Hall, Nottingham, Overlea, Carney, Chase, and surrounding areas. Call us if you're unsure — we likely cover your neighborhood.
Is Drain Pro Plus insured? +
Yes, Drain Pro Plus is fully insured. This protects both you and our team. We carry general liability insurance on all jobs. You can have complete peace of mind that your home and property are protected when we're working there.
What plumbing services do you offer? +
We offer a full range of residential and commercial plumbing services: drain cleaning, plumbing repairs (faucets, toilets, fixtures), pipe installation and replacement, leak detection and repair, sewer line inspection and cleaning, water heater work, and more. If you're not sure we handle something, just call.
How is Drain Pro Plus different from other plumbers? +
When you call Drain Pro Plus, you're often talking directly to Billy — the owner. He personally handles most jobs and treats every customer's home like his own. We're local to Baltimore County, we respond fast, our prices are fair and transparent, and we don't leave until the problem is solved. Our 5-star reviews across Google and Facebook speak for themselves.
What should I do if I have a plumbing emergency? +
First, turn off the water supply to the affected area (or your main shutoff if needed). Then call us immediately at (410) 555-0123. We prioritize urgent situations and will do our best to get someone to you as quickly as possible. Don't wait — water damage compounds quickly.
How do I know if I need drain cleaning vs. a bigger repair? +
Slow drains and minor clogs are usually solved with professional drain cleaning. Multiple slow drains throughout the house, gurgling sounds, or sewage smells can indicate a main line issue that needs a camera inspection. If your drains are backing up with sewage, call us right away — that needs immediate attention.
